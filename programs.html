Our Programs & Initiatives

Comprehensive support systems designed to empower African creatives through mental health advocacy, skill development, community building, and economic opportunities.

Our Core Pillars

Mental Health Advocacy & Wellness Support

Comprehensive mental health awareness campaigns, therapy access, and wellness circles specifically designed for creatives. We provide culturally-relevant support that understands the unique challenges facing African artists and creative professionals.

Creative Empowerment & Capacity Building

Workshops, mentorships, and training programs designed to build skills and enhance creative output. From technical skills to business development, we help creatives grow professionally while maintaining their artistic integrity.

Tech for Mental Health

Development of the CreativeConnect mental health app and digital resource hub for easy, affordable, and culturally-relevant mental health support accessible across Africa.

Community & Advocacy

Grassroots engagement and policy advocacy to integrate mental health into Africa's creative economy. We work to create systemic change that supports creative professionals at all levels.

Economic Inclusion & Funding Access

Grants, incubation, and business support for creative entrepreneurs and projects. We help bridge the gap between creative talent and economic opportunities across the continent.
