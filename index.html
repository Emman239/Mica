01

What we create is built to connect

Our services are crafted to turn ideas into transformative experiences, made with precision and care for the African creative community.

Mental Health Advocacy

We provide comprehensive mental health support, awareness campaigns, therapy access, and wellness circles specifically designed for creatives.

Creative Empowerment

Through workshops, mentorships, and training programs, we build skills and enhance creative output across Africa.

Community Building

We create safe, inclusive spaces for African creatives to connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Our Vision

Building resilient creative communities

To build a mentally resilient and economically empowered creative community across Africa.

Our Mission

Creating safe spaces for growth

To create safe, inclusive, and sustainable spaces for African creatives to thrive through mental health advocacy, creative empowerment, and access to growth opportunities.

02

Our Core Pillars

Mental Health Advocacy

Providing mental health awareness campaigns, therapy access, and wellness circles for creatives.

Creative Empowerment

Workshops, mentorships, and training programs designed to build skills and enhance creative output.

Tech for Mental Health

Development of the CreativeConnect mental health app and digital resource hub.

Community & Advocacy

Grassroots engagement and policy advocacy to integrate mental health into Africa's creative economy.

Economic Inclusion

Grants, incubation, and business support for creative entrepreneurs and projects.
