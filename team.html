Our Leadership Approach

Creative Connect Africa is led by a diverse team of mental health advocates, creative professionals, and program managers with cross-sectoral expertise. Our collaborative approach ensures that we understand both the creative industry landscape and the mental health challenges facing African creatives.

Mr. John Eloka

Founder & Advocate Director

John is a visionary leader who combines his expertise as a visual storyteller with his passion for mental health advocacy. As a creative entrepreneur himself, he understands firsthand the unique challenges that artists face in balancing creative expression with mental wellness. His leadership has been instrumental in establishing Creative Connect Africa as a leading voice for mental health support in the African creative community.

Under his direction, the organization has grown from a concept incubated at Mind Art Display Studio to a registered NGO making tangible impact across the continent. John's approach emphasizes the intersection of creativity and healing, recognizing that mental wellness is not just about treating problems, but about creating environments where creativity can flourish.
